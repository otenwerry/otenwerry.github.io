Hello!

My name is Owen. I’m currently a Research Scholar at MATS, where I study long-context alignment drift in LLM agents. Previously, I researched out-of-distribution propensity generalization in LLMs at SPAR, and studied computer science and math at Columbia University.

I am broadly interested in the alignment of advanced AI, both as a philosophical question (what does it mean for AI to be aligned?) and an empirical research agenda (how do we build AIs that are aligned?).

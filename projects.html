Swipe Market

April 2025 | co-founded with Caroline Smyth

swipemarketcu.com is an online marketplace for Columbia students to buy and sell meal swipes. Dining hall users often end up with lots of leftover swipes on their accounts, and many upperclassmen have no meal plan at all and are willing to buy individual swipes at discounted rates.

Lion Dine

October 2024 | co-founded with Caroline Smyth

liondine.com displays all the Columbia Dining menus on one page to let students compare options instantly, rather than having to click through 11 separate menus on dining.columbia.edu and dineoncampus.com/barnard/whats-on-the-menu . Over 2,000 users per day out of ~7,500 meal plan holders. Over half a million total views.

Can Arctic sea ice melting lead to more summer heat extremes?

August 2024

My summer 2024 research project at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory. Published in Geophysical Research Letters. I analyzed data from experiments ran on the CESM1 climate model to isolate the effects of melting Arctic sea ice on extreme summer temperatures, and to identify the mechanisms by which these effects take place. I worked in Lamont's Ocean & Climate Physics division under the mentorship of Professor Yutian Wu.

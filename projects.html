Research

I’m currently researching value drift in LLM agents with long-term memory, mentored by Maksym Andriushchenko as part of the MATS 10.0 cohort. Before MATS, I researched out-of-distribution propensity generalization in LLMs, mentored by Niels Warncke through SPAR. See Google Scholar for more.

Lion Dine and Swipe Market

liondine.com is the de facto place for the Columbia student body to check food options. Over 4,000 individual users per day out of ~7,500 meal plan holders. We scrape data for eleven Columbia and Barnard dining halls and display it all on one page, so that users don’t have to click through separate URLs for each location. Columbia later changed their website to look more like ours. Built with Caroline Smyth and acquired by Spectator Publishing Company.

swipemarketcu.com lets Columbia students buy and sell meal swipes. Thousands of total transactions. Also built with Caroline Smyth and now managed by Columbia Software Solutions.

Lion Dine

liondine.com is the de facto place for the Columbia student body to check food options. Displays all the Columbia Dining menus on one page so users don't have to click through 11 separate Columbia Dining and Barnard Dining menus. Over 4,000 individual users per day out of ~7,500 meal plan holders. Built with Caroline Smyth in October 2024 and acquired by Spectator Publishing Company in January 2026.

Swipe Market

swipemarketcu.com lets Columbia students buy and sell meal swipes. Built in April 2025, also with Caroline. Thousands of transactions.

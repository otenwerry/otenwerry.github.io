Lion Dine

October 2024 | website | built in collaboration with Caroline Smyth

liondine.com is a website that scrapes food data from the official Columbia Dining and Barnard Dining websites and puts it all on one page. Instead of having to click through multiple pages of multiple slow websites, Columbia students can see what's available all at once.

Can Arctic sea ice melting lead to more summer heat extremes?

August 2024 | paper | poster

My summer 2024 research project at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory. I analyzed data from experiments ran on the CESM1 climate model to isolate the effects of melting Arctic sea ice on extreme summer temperatures, and to identify the mechanisms by which these effects take place. I worked in Lamont's Ocean & Climate Physics division under the mentorship of Professor Yutian Wu.

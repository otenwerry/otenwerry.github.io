Lion Dine

liondine.com is a website that scrapes food data from the official Columbia Dining and Barnard Dining websites and puts it all on one page. Instead of having to click through multiple pages of multiple slow websites, Columbia students can see what's available all at once.

Can Arctic sea ice melting lead to more summer heat extremes?

My summer 2024 research project at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory. I analyzed data from experiments ran on the CESM1 climate model to isolate the effects of melting Arctic sea ice on extreme summer temperatures, and to identify the mechanisms by which these effects take place. I worked in Lamont's Ocean & Climate Physics division and was mentored by Professor Yutian Wu.
